Why we exist

The reason the company exists.

We exist because too many Indian patients accept surgery they do not need, recover from surgery slower than they should, and live with pain longer than they have to.

Every role at Avana — sales, marketing, operations, finance, service — moves a real person closer to a better outcome. That is not a poster on the wall; it is the reason the company exists.

Working here

What working here is actually like.

At Avana, our success is driven by the passion and dedication of our people. We are a growing company that offers a dynamic and supportive work environment, with real opportunities for career advancement and personal growth. Here, employees don't just fill roles — they are encouraged to take an active part in shaping the future of the company and making a positive impact on the lives of patients and their families.

Discover Your Purpose. Avana is more than just a workplace; it is a platform for personal and professional growth. We believe in the power of purpose and offer you the opportunity to make a meaningful impact in the field of medical devices. Together, we revolutionize healthcare, improve patient outcomes, and create a legacy that inspires future generations.

Unleash Your Creativity. We celebrate diversity, encourage innovation, and foster an environment where your unique ideas and perspectives thrive. Your voice matters here, and your creativity will shape the future of medical devices. We provide the resources, mentorship, and freedom to explore uncharted territories — to push boundaries, challenge norms, and bring groundbreaking solutions to life.

Grow Beyond Limits. We are committed to your growth and development. We offer continuous learning, skill enhancement, and career advancement through comprehensive medical education programs, industry partnerships, and access to cutting-edge technologies. With us, you will constantly evolve, conquer new challenges, and unlock your full potential.

Join an Inspiring Community. Working alongside passionate colleagues and inspiring leaders is what sets life at Avana apart. Our distinctive culture of collaboration and support means you are guided and empowered at every step of your journey — whether you are just starting out or managing complex portfolios.

Make a Difference. By joining Avana, you become part of a movement that has a profound impact on healthcare. Our work directly touches the lives of patients — over 40,000 happy patients and 500+ trained surgeons supported so far — bringing hope, healing, and innovation to those who need it most.

Recognizing Your Value. We appreciate and acknowledge the value every team member brings. How we hire

A fair, fast, and transparent process.

1

You apply.

Apply to a specific role that interests you. Don't see the right fit? Write to careers@avanasurgical.com and tell us what you do well and what you'd like to do here.

2

We get in touch.

If you're shortlisted, our HR team will reach out to discuss the role and what we're looking for.

3

Virtual interview.

A video call with our HR Head to get to know you better.

4

In-person interview.

A face-to-face conversation with the hiring manager or MD.

5

Offer letter.

If you're selected, we'll send you the offer.
