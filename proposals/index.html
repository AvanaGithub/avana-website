Why is "For Surgeons" missing?

The /audiences/surgeon page has a different audience and a different commercial flow — it's a B2B partnership page, not a patient-education page. It's getting its own treatment plan rather than the patient-friendly template used by the 7 samples above.

How to review

  1. Open each sample and scroll the page top-to-bottom as a real patient would.
  2. At the bottom of each sample you'll find a "What's changing on this page" block — a side-by-side comparison of the current meta, sections, word counts, and SEO wins.
  3. Mark any concerns — wording, tone, clinical accuracy, missing context — and send to info@avanasurgical.com.
  4. Once approved, the new content moves into the live data/conditions/*.json and data/audiences/*.json files, and the actual pages on the site update automatically.

Privacy: All sample pages have noindex, nofollow tags so Google won't index them. They're publicly viewable to anyone with the URL but won't appear in search results. Delete after approval if you'd prefer they disappear entirely.
