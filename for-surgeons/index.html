Surgeon Training & Education

Fostering expertise and skill — Avana's pledge to medical professionals' growth.

Education is a lifelong journey. Avana's medical education programmes give surgeons the latest techniques in Full Endoscopic Spine Surgery, hands-on cadaver and bone model practice, and a network of colleagues across India. Find the workshop that's right for you below.

Courses & Workshops

Training designed around your practice

From hands-on surgical workshops and cadaver labs to symposia and CME lectures — we run programmes at every stage of a surgeon's career, with an emphasis on Full Endoscopic Spine Surgery and minimally invasive techniques.

🦴

Cadaver Workshops

Multi-station cadaver labs at AIIMS, MS Ramaiah, Symbiosis and other leading centres. Practise techniques on tissue under faculty guidance.

🧠

Bone Model Training

Hands-on bone model stations with camera systems — ideal for residents, fellows, and early-career spine surgeons building procedure confidence.

🔬

Live Surgery Demonstrations

Observe full-endoscopic spine procedures performed live by senior faculty, with intra-operative commentary and case discussion.

💻

Webinars & KOL Series

Online lectures from key opinion leaders — "KOL to Young Surgeon" series and patient-education webinars. Free to register, accessible across India.

        Training programme FAQs

        Our programmes are designed for practising orthopaedic surgeons, spine surgeons, neurosurgeons, and senior residents/fellows. Cadaver workshops typically have a minimum experience level set by the host faculty — check the specific event listing.

        We have both free and paid training programs, check the program page for more details. Conference-hosted programmes (ASSICON, KOACON, GESS, etc.) follow the host society's registration model — contact us and we'll connect you to the right organiser.

        Click "Register / Enquire" on any upcoming programme card below, or email info@avanasurgical.com with your name, hospital, and the programme you're interested in. Our team will confirm a slot and share venue details.

        Yes — we partner with medical colleges and hospitals across India to host bone-model and cadaver workshops. We bring the systems, faculty support, and equipment; you provide the venue and the resident cohort. Email us to discuss.

        Some Avana programmes are CME-accredited via the host institution or society. Where applicable, the specific event listing will note the credit hours. Webinars are typically informational; in-person conference sessions often carry credits.

        Avana's flagship programmes cover the full curriculum: anatomy and approach, decompression techniques, fusion, and post-op management. We use RIWO Spine systems and follow the protocols of leading international centres, adapted for the Indian operating environment.

        Several of our programmes include live-surgery viewing days at the host hospital. These have limited slots — register early. Online streamed live surgeries are also part of the KOL-to-Young-Surgeon webinar series.
