Simple by Design

How the Kodiak Works

Set up in minutes. Operate with confidence. Recover with purpose.

01
Fill with Ice & Water
Add ice and water to the reservoir. A small amount provides hours of consistent cold therapy delivery.
02
Attach Intelli-Flo® Pad
Connect the anatomically designed pad for your specific treatment area — knee, shoulder, ankle, hip, or back.
03
Apply with Barrier
Place an insulation barrier between the pad and skin per instructions. Sterile dressings are available for each pad type.
04
Power On & Recover
Simply plug in and begin therapy. Whisper-quiet motorized cold therapy runs continuously for 6–8 hours per fill.
