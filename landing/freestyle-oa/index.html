There's often a step between painkillers and a knee replacement.

Most people with knee osteoarthritis are told two things — manage the pain, and wait until it's bad enough for surgery. But for many, there's a middle step that can reduce the pain of daily movement and help postpone surgery, sometimes by months or more.

Meet the Freestyle OA Brace.

In knee osteoarthritis, most of the pain comes from one worn side of the knee carrying too much load. The Freestyle OA brace gently shifts that load away from the painful side — so walking, standing and stairs feel easier. It's lightweight, worn under or over clothing, and made by Breg, a global leader in orthopedic bracing.
